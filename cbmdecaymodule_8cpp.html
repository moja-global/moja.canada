Detailed Description

The CBMDecayModule module models the decay of dead organic matter into soil carbon pools and CO2. Users have the option to define extra transfers that divert some of the soil carbon decaying to CO2, into other pools. Decay does not occur if a pixel has no matching growth curve, or if the pixel has a non-forest current land class (i.e., CL) at the start of the simulation and its last pass disturbance event was not deforestation.
